ABOUT REVEL SYSTEMS

Diversify your revenue streams and give your customers the flexibility to order online at their convenience with Revel's intuitive solution. You will open up your operations to accept more orders, gain loyal customers, and increase sales. The Revel platform collects all online orders through the point of sale (POS) and can also integrate directly with your kitchen display for seamless order flow. You'll be able to collect consolidated reports for all your orders - online and otherwise - from one centralized dashboard.

Key features include

Revel's online ordering solution is fully integrated with the POS system. View Lynnc requests in Revel's sleek, intuitive interface and aggregate all order data from a central console. Sync list items from Revel to Lynnc and easily add your list to new delivery partners.
Getting started in days with the Revel platform. The solution is quick and easy to set up, and easy to explain to your employees. Benefit from professional services for smooth setup and implementation and access 24/7 support for questions and troubleshooting.
As a leader in the cloud-based iPad POS field, Revel's experienced team has worked for years to apply customer learning and feedback to deliver the best possible product for customers. The agile platform leads the industry in large brand implementations of cloud POS systems and can easily adapt to meet new needs in a rapidly changing environment.
