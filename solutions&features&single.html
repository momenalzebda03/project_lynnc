Streamline Your Order Management Process with Lynnc

Manually copying online orders from various devices to your own point of sale is time-consuming and leads to mistakes, so we automate that task - aggregating all your orders and synchronizing with your in-house system.

Organization and Automation

Lynnc helps you organize and automate key parts of the buying/delivery process for you and your customer. After placing an order and choosing a delivery method, an automated process of fulfilment is triggered. Whether shipping from the nearest and/or most cost-effective location, or preparing the item for in-store pickup, Lynnc's platform helps fulfil orders quickly.
